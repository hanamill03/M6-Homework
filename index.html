Kamakura

Kamakura (鎌倉) is a coastal town in Kanagawa Prefecture, less than an hour south of Tokyo.Today, Kamakura is a small city and a very popular tourist destination. Sometimes called the Kyoto of eastern Japan, Kamakura offers numerous temples, shrines and other historical monuments. In addition, Kamakura's sand beaches attract large crowds during the summer.
